Item 2.02. Results of Operations and Financial Condition.
Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure.

 

On October 8, 2020, IBM issued a press release announcing its intention to separate its Managed Infrastructure Services unit of its Global Technology Services division into a new public company. The Company is hosting an investor webcast at 8:00 a.m. Eastern Time on October 8, 2020 relating to the separation.

 

The press release also included preliminary financial results for the quarter ended September 30, 2020.  As the Company is currently in its normal financial closing process, these are approximate figures and are subject to revision until the Company reports its full third-quarter results as planned later this month.

 

Copies of the press release and the presentation materials are attached hereto as Exhibits 99.1 and 99.2, respectively, and incorporated by reference herein.

 

In an effort to provide investors with additional information regarding the Company’s results as determined by generally accepted accounting principles (GAAP), the Company has disclosed in the attached press release and presentation materials certain non-GAAP information which management believes provides useful information to investors. Reconciliations of these non-GAAP financial measures to the most directly comparable financial measures calculated and presented in accordance with GAAP are included in the press release, presentation materials and non-GAAP financial information, which are Exhibits 99.1, 99.2 and 99.3, respectively, to this Form 8-K. The rationale for management’s use of non-GAAP measures is included in Exhibit 99.3 to this Form 8-K.

 

The information in Items 2.02 and 7.01 of this Form 8-K, including the corresponding Exhibits 99.1, 99.2 and 99.3, is being furnished with the Commission and shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934.
