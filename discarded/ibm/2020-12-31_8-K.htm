Item 8.01. Other Events.

 

On December 24, 2020, IBM Credit LLC (“IBM Credit”), a wholly-owned subsidiary of International Business Machines Corporation ("IBM"), entered into a Receivables Purchase Agreement (the “Agreement”) with Banco Santander S.A., pursuant to which IBM Credit may sell up to $3 billion of its IBM short-term commercial financing receivables at any one time, on a revolving basis. The transfers of these receivables are expected to qualify as true sales and will begin in December in the United States and Canada. Sales are expected to expand to certain other countries through the first half of 2021. The initial term of the Agreement is three years.
