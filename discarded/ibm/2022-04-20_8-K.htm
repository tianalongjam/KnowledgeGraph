Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure.

​

Exhibit 99.1 of this Form 8-K contains the prepared remarks for IBM’s Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Arvind Krishna and Chief Financial Officer Jim Kavanaugh’s first-quarter 2022 earnings presentation to investors on April 19, 2022.

​

Reconciliations of non-GAAP financial measures discussed in the earnings presentation to the most directly comparable financial measures calculated and presented in accordance with GAAP are included as Exhibit 99.2 of this Form 8-K, and include the updated slide on the company’s expectation for pre-tax margin improvement from the second quarter of 2021 to the second quarter of 2022 that was Exhibit 99.1 to the Amended Form 8-K submitted to the SEC on April 20, 2022 (the “Updated Slide”). The slides used in Messrs. Krishna and Kavanaugh’s first-quarter 2022 earnings presentation are Exhibit 99.3 to the company’s Form 8-K submitted to the SEC on April 19, 2022, which were corrected with the Updated Slide.

​

The information in this Item 7.01, including the corresponding Exhibits 99.1 and 99.2, is being furnished with the Commission and shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (the “Exchange Act”), with the exception of the following information from the question and answer period of the earnings presentation, which has been edited to provide additional context and clarity, and shall be deemed “filed” under the Exchange Act:

​

●The company expects a sequential increase of approximately $1.1 billion in revenue from the first quarter of 2022 to the second quarter of 2022. This is approximately $200 million more than our historical first quarter to second quarter sequential increase, driven by the launch of the IBM z16 system late in the second quarter of 2022, mitigated by a currency headwind due to the U.S. dollar strengthening.
